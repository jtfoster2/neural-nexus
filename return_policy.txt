Return Eligibility:
We accept returns on most new, unused, and resalable items within 60 days of delivery. Items must be in original packaging with all accessories and documentation. Products must not show signs of wear, damage, or alteration. Proof of purchase is required. Non-returnable items include digital goods, perishable items, personalized items, gift cards, and clearance or final sale items.

Return Categories:
Electronics:
Electronics must be returned within 30 days. Items must be in original retail packaging with all cables, chargers, manuals, and accessories. Devices must not be registered, paired, or linked to any account. No physical damage, modifications, or liquid exposure. Items with removed serial numbers or tampered security seals cannot be returned. Open batteries and modified software items are not eligible. Factory reset is required when applicable. A restocking fee of up to 15% may apply if the item is not in like-new condition.

Apparel and Wearables:
Clothing and accessories must be unworn, unwashed, and free of stains, odors, or pet hair. All original tags must be attached. Footwear must be returned in the original shoe box, placed inside a shipping box. Undergarments, swimwear, or intimate wear that has been opened cannot be returned.

Footwear:
Shoes must show no signs of outdoor wear. The shoe box cannot be used as the shipping box.

Home Goods:
Includes décor, kitchenware, and small furnishings. Items must be unused and undamaged. Large items must be returned disassembled if originally shipped that way. Transit damage must be reported with photos within 7 days of delivery.

Accessories (Non-Electronics):
Includes wallets, belts, bags, and tools. Items must be unused and unaltered.

Consumables:
Consumable items such as cleaning products, oils, coatings, or similar items are non-returnable once opened unless defective.

Bundles and Kits:
All items in a bundle must be returned together. Individual items from a bundle cannot be returned separately.

RETURN PROCESS

Submit a Request:
Use your account or the Returns Center to provide your order number and reason for return.

Approval:
Our team reviews requests within 2–3 business days. Approved requests receive an RMA and, if eligible, a prepaid return label.

Pack Your Item:
Package items securely, preferably in original packaging. Remove or cover all old shipping labels.

Ship It Back:
Return shipments must be dropped off within 7 days of RMA approval.

RETURN SHIPPING COSTS

Defective or incorrect items: We cover all return shipping costs.

Customer-initiated returns (changed mind): Customer pays return shipping.

Exchanges: One free replacement shipment per original order.

REFUNDS

Refunds are issued 5–10 business days after the return is received and inspected.

Refund timeline by payment type:

Credit/Debit Cards: 5–10 business days

PayPal or Digital Wallets: 1–3 business days

Store Credit: Issued instantly once approved

Possible deductions include shipping fees and restocking charges up to 15% for non-defective returns.

DAMAGED, DEFECTIVE, OR INCORRECT ITEMS

If you receive a defective or incorrect item, contact customer support within 7 days with photos of the product and packaging. We will prioritize replacements or full refunds.

EXCHANGES

We offer exchanges for the same item in a different color or size if available. For different items, place a new order after your refund is processed.

INTERNATIONAL RETURNS

International customers must pay all return shipping fees, duties, and taxes. Refunds are in USD and may vary due to currency changes.

CANCELLATIONS

Orders may be canceled within 2 hours if they have not yet shipped. Once shipped, the regular return process applies.

WARRANTY POLICY

Some products include a 1-year limited warranty against manufacturer defects. Warranty claims require proof of purchase and serial number verification. Damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear is not covered.
